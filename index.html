Practice SSC History with a structured 100-day plan

History 100-Day Finisher is an independent educational practice app for history topics commonly asked in competitive exams.

The app uses a fixed learning structure called the Syllabus Exhaustion Protocol. Every learner follows the same progression across a fixed question pool.

  • 100 days total
  • 32 MCQs per day
  • 3200 total questions
  • 16 modules and 160 subtopics

How the plan works

The protocol divides the history syllabus into a balanced sequence of daily practice sessions. Each day focuses on a specific domain of the syllabus while maintaining a consistent workload.

Over 100 days, the question pool is practiced through a structured progression.

Target Exams

  • SSC related exams (CGL, CHSL, MTS)
  • Banking exams (IBPS PO, Clerk)
  • Railway recruitment exams
