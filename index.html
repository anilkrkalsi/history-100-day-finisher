Finish SSC History in 100 Days.

History 100-Day Finisher is a fixed-pool history preparation system built on the Syllabus Exhaustion Protocol using the Standard 3200 Core. No bloat. No endless content expansion. One defined pool. One defined route.

Product Identity

  • App name: History 100-Day Finisher
  • Method: Syllabus Exhaustion Protocol
  • Core: Standard 3200 Core
  • Promise: Finish SSC History in 100 Days

Engine Rules

  • 100 days
  • 32 MCQs per day
  • Total 3200 questions
  • Fixed pool — no silent additions
  • 1 day unlock every 24 hours
  • No skipping forward

Distribution

  • Modern: 50%
  • Ancient: 15%
  • Medieval: 15%
  • Art & Culture: 20%

Scoring Logic

Canonical Accuracy = (Correct First Attempts ÷ 3200) × 100

Only first attempts count toward the canonical record. Reattempts are for revision, not for rewriting performance history.

Eligibility Rule

  • At least 75% overall canonical accuracy
  • At least 60% in Ancient
  • At least 60% in Medieval
  • At least 60% in Modern

Art & Culture contributes to total performance, but the locked sectional floor applies to Ancient, Medieval, and Modern.

Why this exists

Most exam-prep products keep expanding content and destroying trust. This system does the opposite. It defines the pool, defines the schedule, and forces measurable completion.

This is not an entertainment app. It is a completion instrument.
