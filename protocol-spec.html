Protocol Specification

Core Rules

  • Fixed pool: 3200 questions
  • Duration: 100 days
  • Daily load: 32 MCQs
  • No forward skipping
  • One unlock every 24 hours

Distribution Rules

  • Modern: 50%
  • Ancient: 15%
  • Medieval: 15%
  • Art & Culture: 20%

Scoring

Canonical Accuracy = (Correct First Attempts ÷ 3200) × 100

Canonical Accuracy uses only the first attempt for each question. Reattempts do not overwrite the canonical ledger.

Eligibility

  • ≥75% overall canonical accuracy
  • ≥60% Ancient
  • ≥60% Medieval
  • ≥60% Modern

Behavioral Contract

  • The user cannot skip ahead to unopened days.
  • The question pool is fixed and should not silently expand.
  • Progression is day-locked, not binge-unlocked.
  • The product is built for measurable syllabus completion, not endless browsing.
