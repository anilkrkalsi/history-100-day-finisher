FAQ

Is this an endless question bank?

No. The pool is fixed at Standard 3200 Core.

How many questions per day?

32 MCQs per day for 100 days.

Can I skip ahead?

No. Progress unlocks one day at a time every 24 hours.

What counts in score?

Only first attempts count toward Canonical Accuracy.

Will new questions keep getting added?

No. The Standard 3200 Core is a fixed pool. Silent expansion breaks trust.

What is the eligibility rule?

Eligibility requires at least 75% overall canonical accuracy and at least 60% in Ancient, Medieval, and Modern.

Is this made for entertainment-style practice?

No. This product is built as a completion instrument, not as a scrolling content app.
